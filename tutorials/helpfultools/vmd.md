Aligning structures with unequal number of atoms or different atom numbers.
1. Open RMSD calculator
2. Enter the command:

```
(resid 60 and resname DC3) or (resid 52 and resname DC3)
```

Aligning trajectories 
1. RMSD Trajectory
2. Choose mask for alignment
